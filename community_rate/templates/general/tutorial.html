Welcome to CommunityRate!

The site where your friends' opinions matter, not the whole worlds'.

Ever wanted to share your thoughts on movies with your friends? Keep track of what movies you want to watch? Figure out what movie to watch next?

If so, you've come to the right place!
Already know what's up?
Never been here before?

Tutorial step 1/2: It all starts with the nav bar (side edition)

As you can now see to the left, the side nav bar is revealed. This is what you will use to navigate through the site.

The navbar has several options:

  • If you click on your name, you will be able to view your profile, change settings, or logout
  • The Activity Feed will present the most recent reviews all of your friends have made.
  • Below is another, more easily accessible link to your Profile
  • Under the Users tab, you will be able to view a list of all users to find new friends, or view the users you already have relationships with
  • With the Lists functionality, you'll be able to create lists of movies and keep them private or share with others
  • Finally, the Movie Database is where you'll be able to search for any movie to give it a review or see what your friends thought

Tutorial step 2/2: It all continues with the nav bar (top edition)

At the top of the page, the top nav bar is now visible. This nav bar has additional options to help you out.

This navbar has the following options:

  • You can search for movies from anywhere within the site
  • Any notifications (such as when a user follows you) will be displayed in a drop down menu
  • Your user options such as profile, settings, and logout are made available again

In addition, in the top left corner the general section of the site you're currently on will be displayed.
