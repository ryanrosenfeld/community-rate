Help - Instructions, Tips, and Common Questions

  • How do I rate movies?
  • How can I share lists with friends?
  • Why is my activity feed blank?
To start rating movies, follow these 3 easy steps:
  1. Search for a movie, either in the search bar at the top of the page or in the Movie Database
  2. Go to the movie page for the movie you want to rate by clicking on the movie's row
  3. Click the Update Review button in the My Review section on the right side of the page to enter a rating!
Click on the Lists link on the side nav bar to go to the main Lists page. From here, you have 3 views:
  1. My Lists: all the lists you have made
  2. Popular Lists: the most popular public lists your friends have made
  3. Editable Lists: the lists your friends have given you permission to edit
Out of the gate it's really easy to share public lists with friends, as you can automatically see all your friends' lists and like them if they're really cool. But, this can be taken even further

By clicking on the Permissions button on one of your lists, you can add Editors to your list. Any friends you add as Editors can also edit the list, so now you can create awesome group lists with friends!
The activity feed only shows the movie reviews of the people you follow. Either you aren't following any other users, or the users you are following haven't reviewed anything yet.

Start following users, or tell your friends to get active and start making ratings!
